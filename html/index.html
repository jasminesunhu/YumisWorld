FAQ

  • Q: How can I care for my crochet jewelry?

    A: Avoid getting your jewelry wet. Water can weaken the yarn and make it lose its shape.
    Store your jewelry in a cool, dry place. Avoid direct sunlight, as it can fade the colors.
    Gently spot clean any dirt with a damp cloth. Don't submerge the jewelry or use harsh chemicals.
    For pieces with metal findings, you can polish them with a soft cloth.
